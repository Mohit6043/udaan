About the event

Udaan Club- The Entrepreneurship club of management department and Department of Management Studies putting forward a much-needed webinar "Life Lessons From My 50 years Corporate Career". An encouraging talk by Mr. Pradeep Kashyap, a former TEDx speaker and Founder & CEO of "MART".


This talk comprises key insights and lessons from his life's professional and personal journey. This is going to be no less awaring and motivational for aspiring youth in corporate and entrepreneurship field.

our speaker

Mr. Pradeep Kashyap

Founder & CEO, MART

Mr. Pradeep Kashyap (Founder & CEO, MART) (Author of the Book 'Rural Marketing'; Titled 'Father of Rural India' by Prof. C K Prahalad) He founded MART in 1993, a rural marketing consultancy firm working in emerging markets with a vision to become the most respected employee-owned consultancy firm creating innovative and substantial solutions for the poor. MART is a ‘Business Mind, Social Heart’ organization based on spiritual principles and family values. They have conceived several new concepts, like ‘micro-finance to mirco-enterprise’, ‘public private partnership’ for development sector and ‘rural marketing’ as an important growth strategy for corporate. He introduced the subject of "Rural Marketing” in Business Management Schools in India and Bangladesh. and has also authored the most comprehensive 'Rural Marketing' book, for students and practitioners alike.
